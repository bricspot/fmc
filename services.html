Family Planning Services

Our Family Planning unit provides comprehensive reproductive health services tailored to your individual needs and lifestyle. Our trained nurses and clinical officers offer confidential, non-judgmental consultations to help you make informed decisions about your reproductive health.

  • Short-term Methods: Oral contraceptive pills, injectable contraceptives (Depo-Provera), and emergency contraception
  • Long-term Methods: Intrauterine devices (IUDs - Copper T & Mirena), and Implants (Jadelle, Implanon)
  • Permanent Methods: Counselling and referral for tubal ligation and vasectomy
  • Fertility Awareness: Natural family planning education and cycle tracking guidance
  • Pre-conception Care: Health assessments and counselling for couples planning pregnancy

Specialist: Nurse Mary Wanjiru (Certified Family Planning Counsellor) & Clinical Officer Peter Mwangi
