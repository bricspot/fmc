Our Story

A Decade of Service

Founded over 10 years ago in Kerugoya, Focus Clinical started as a small diagnostic laboratory with a big ambition: to bridge the gap in specialist medical services in the region.

Today, we have grown into a network of 8 branches across Kerugoya (3 branches), Murang'a (2 branches), Embu, Mwea, and Ishiara. Our Kerugoya and Murang'a laboratories are ISO 15189:2022 accredited, reflecting our commitment to international quality standards. We've stayed true to our roots, providing personalized care that treats every patient like family, while continuously investing in the latest medical technology.
