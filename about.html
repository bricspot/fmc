Our Story

A Decade of Service

Founded over 10 years ago in Murang'a Town, Focus Clinical started as a small diagnostic laboratory with a big ambition: to bridge the gap in specialist medical services in the region.

Today, we have grown into a multi-branch network covering Murang'a, Kerugoya, Embu, and Ishiara. We've stayed true to our roots, providing personalized care that treats every patient like family, while continuously investing in the latest medical technology.
